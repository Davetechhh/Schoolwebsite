Welcome to Rufus Giwa Polytechnic Owo Application Form Portal

GUIDELINES

Rugipo Application Form Notice Board

  • Candidates who made Rufus Giwa Polytechnic Owo their first choice in the UTME and scored 110 and above, and those who seeking admission into PGD URP/HND Fulltime/Part Time Programmes can now register for the screening exercise.
  • Candidates should please note that they may experience occasional SERVER LOAD/NETWORK Challenges. When this happens, please wait for 30 minutes and try again..
  • Candidates are required to go through the following processes:
    Step 1: Visit https://elearning.rugipo.edu.ng/Rugipo_Application_Portal/
    Step 2: Log on to the site by Clicking on Application Form.
    Step 3: Choose Form Option and click Save Redirect to launch Screening Form Registration Page.
    Step 4: Fill the Screening Form Registration Page and click on Submit.
    Step 5: A page Invoice will appear showing your FULL NAME, REGISTRATION/APPLICATION NO,DEPARTMENT, LEVEL AND AMOUNT TO PAY. CLICK ON PAY
    Step 6: Insert your ATM Details (Only Master/Visa Card is advisable). Click on Make Payment
    Step 7:. After making payment, Print your Receipt
    Step 8: Complete filling of the form. Save and Print the Form


  • 2: PAYMENT RECEIPT NOT DISPLAYING
    1. Clicking on Reprint Slip
    2. Follow Instructions thereafter to print your Receipt
    3. Enter your Application/JAMB No
    3. Select Payment Reprint Slip as Event
    3. Payment Receipt is displayed
    4. Print the Receipt
    5. Done


  • 3: APPLICATION FORM NOT SAVED
    1. Clicking on Reprint Slip
    2. Follow Instructions thereafter to print your Receipt
    3. Enter your Application/JAMB No
    3. Select Payment Reprint Slip as Event
    3. Payment Receipt is displayed
    4. Click on Fill Form Now
    5. Filled Form will be displayed but ENSURE YOUR PASSPORT SIZE IS NOT MORE THAN 6kb
    6. Done


  • 4: UPLOAD O'LEVEL RESULTS/EDIT PARAMETERS
    1. Clicking on Upload O'Level/Edit Parameters
    2. Enter the JambNumber/Application Number
    3. Click on Load Parameters
    4. Enter the necessary fields
    3. Click on Save it
    4. Done


  • 5: MONITOR ADMISSION STATUS
    1. Clicking on Monitor Admission Status
    2. Enter the JambNumber/Application Number
    3. Click on login
    3. If Offered Provision, a page showing Congratulatory Message will display
    3. Click Print to Print this Page
    4. Click on Provisional Admission Letter to Display and Print the Page
    5. If not Yet Offered Admission, ensure you upload your O'LEVEL Results and JAMB and O'LEVEL Subjects Combinations meet the basic minimum requirements in your choiced course of study
    6. Apply for Change of Course through JAMB Caps and notify Rufus Giwa Polytechnic by sending your printout via admissions@rugipo.edu.ng for neccesary attention
    7. Done


  • 6: PAY ACCEPTANCE/ADMISSION LEETER/VERIFICATION
    1. Clicking on Pay Acceptance/Admission/Verification
    2. Enter the JambNumber/Application Number against (Acceptance/Admission/Verification)
    3. Click on Pay for Acceptance/Pay for Admission/Pay for Verification
    4. A page Invoice will appear showing your FULL NAME, REGISTRATION/APPLICATION NO,DEPARTMENT, LEVEL AND AMOUNT TO PAY. CLICK ON PAY
    5. Insert your ATM Details (Only Master/Visa Card is advisable). Click on Make Payment
    6: After making payment, Print your Receipt
    7. Done


  • 7: ACCEPTANCE/ADMISSION LEETER/VERIFICATION RECEIPT NOT DISPLAYING CORRECTLY
    1. Clicking on Reprint Fees Receipt
    2. Enter your Application/JAMB No
    3. Select Payment Title from the Drop Down List
    3. Select Status
    3. Click on Generate Receipt
    4. Payment Receipt is displayed
    5. Print the Receipt
    6. Done


  • 8: FALSIFICATION OF RESULTS
    1. Rufus Giwa Polytechnic has ZERO tolerance for result falsification and examination malpractice. Applicants who provide false information, or upload incorrect credentials will be automatically disqualified


  • 9: CONTACT US
    All enquiries should be forwarded to admissions@rugipo.edu.ng or enquiry@rugipo.edu.ng.Thank you
